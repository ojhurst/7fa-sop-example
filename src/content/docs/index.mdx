## What this is

A starter kit for the internal wiki every seven-figure agency eventually wishes it had built sooner. Forty SOP stubs across paid, organic, sales, onboarding, operations, measurement, and creative. Fork it, rename it, start filling in your version of the truth.

## How to use it

1. Clone this repo.
2. Rename it to something that means something to your team.
3. Open any stub, delete the TODO, write down how you actually do it.
4. Push. Teach your team to look here first.
